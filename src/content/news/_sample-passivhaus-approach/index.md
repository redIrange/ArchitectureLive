The Passivhaus standard originated in Germany and focuses on building fabric performance above all
else. By reducing heat loss through super-insulation, thermal-bridge-free construction and
mechanical ventilation with heat recovery, buildings can achieve dramatically lower energy demand.
At ArchitectureLIVE we apply these principles pragmatically across project types and budgets.
