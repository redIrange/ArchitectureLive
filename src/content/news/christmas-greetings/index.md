As 2021 draws to a close, we are grateful to reflect on yet another successful year for ArchitectureLIVE. Despite many a challenges we have been fortunate to work with inspirational clients and resourceful and professional consultants and contractors to progress an exciting array of projects. Thank you for supporting us to make this happen.

Following record levels of funding during the pandemic, regrettably latest homelessness statistics show a concerning rise of people sleeping rough. This Christmas we are therefore supporting the charity Shelter again. If you too would like to donate to this cause, please follow this [link](https://england.shelter.org.uk/donate?reserved_appeal_code=20211101-IG-47&gclid=EAIaIQobChMIscXZuYzZ9AIVgZ-zCh0QzwppEAAYASAAEgJ_XfD_BwE&gclsrc=aw.ds).

We wish you and your family a very Merry Christmas and prosperous 2022!
