Having lovingly renovated, remodelled and extended an original 18th century cottage and its numerous extensions, this project is now drawing to a close. The highly individual interior design with our bespoke furniture is receiving its finishing touches. The holistic design approach we applied to the entire family home also encompasses materials and lighting throughout.

The unmistakable design thread running through the whole country home pays witness to the client’s choices and personality, perfectly executed by the team on-site.

Ambient LED lighting throughout delivers an energy efficient and eco-friendly environment for this sensitive location in the South Downs National Park. Our hard landscaping design and the remaining external works will be completed in parallel with the planting season.
