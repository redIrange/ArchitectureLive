Our award winning [Upside Down House Extension & Reconfiguration](https://www.architecturelive.co.uk/projects/1960s-bungalow-haslemere-surrey/) in Haslemere, Surrey will be featured on [Channel 4](https://www.channel4.com/programmes/extraordinary-extensions), in this new Series of Extraordinary Extensions. 

Presented by Tinie, this second instalment follows yet another set of extraordinary house extensions, exploring the home owners’ experience as the projects evolve. Some [critics](https://inews.co.uk/culture/television/extraordinary-extensions-tinie-tempah-channel-4-review-1282441) say that the fun, maximalist examples chosen lack real purpose. Yet our bungalow extension certainly does not follow that trend.

Our project will be one of the retrospective reviews, aired in Series 2, Episode 4, on the 2nd of February 2024.
