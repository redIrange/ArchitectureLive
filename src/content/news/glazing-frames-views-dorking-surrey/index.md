The glazing at our project in Dorking, Surrey has had a transformative effect. The structural roof light by [Glazing Vision](https://www.glazingvision.co.uk/) and the double slider by [Kloeber](https://www.kloeber.co.uk/) now frame the stunning views of the Surrey Hills.

To read more about this project, click [here](https://www.architecturelive.co.uk/2021/06/a-room-with-a-view-dorking-surrey/).
