Irene Konschill will be back at the Homebuilding & Renovating Show to be held at Farnborough International on Sunday, January 19th.

As an expert for the Royal Institute of British Architects (RIBA), Irene will advise on projects at all stages. Her specific interest is in Passivhaus and energy preservation, so why not pop by and have a chat?

If you would like to attend the show on either January 19th or 20th, we are also able to offer complimentary tickets via this [link](https://homebuildingshow.co.uk/riba). 

For more information, visit the Homebuilding & Renovating Show website [here](https://farnborough.homebuildingshow.co.uk/).
