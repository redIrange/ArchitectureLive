The [Bentley SIPs](https://bentley-sips.com/) team arrived punctually and in good spirits. The delivery of the SIPs - structured insulated panels - had been conscientiously organised after the morning rush hour and went smoothly. Then the icy rain started and the temporary driveway cover turned into slushy mud. Yet the crew persisted, slab levels were checked and sole plates installed.
