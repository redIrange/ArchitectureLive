A planning design has been submitted to West Berkshire Council to replace and upgrade valuable community sports facilities at Browns Meadow in Newbury. ArchitectureLIVE’s proposal is to replace the existing sports pavilion, which has come to the end of its useful life. The design is for a new, like-for-like but contemporary single storey building providing modernised and accessible facilities to comply with current standards and the Equalities Act 2010.
