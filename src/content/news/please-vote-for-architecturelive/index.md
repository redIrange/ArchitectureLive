Following the exciting news that one of our projects has been shortlisted for the 2018 Waverley Design Awards, we would be very grateful if you could support our entry by voting for it in the People's Choice category.

Our design for a single storey rear and side extension to a detached property in Haslemere is one of the smallest of 16 varied projects, several of which are indeed stunning, demonstrating that even a very modest budget can deliver huge benefits for clients, if well designed.

The voting process is simple, visit [www.waverley.gov.uk/designawards](https://waverley-self.achieveservice.com/en/AchieveForms/?form_uri=sandbox-publish://AF-Process-8e587446-9770-4b85-bcd3-466bd4b279b2/AF-Stage-af823307-3c71-41e0-a6eb-dd56746bcdf5/definition.json&redirectlink=%2Fen&cancelRedirectLink=%2Fen&consentMessage=yes), complete the online form and then scroll down to our project “Extension to dwelling, Haslemere” to vote.

You will have to give a reason for voting, so if you would like to find out more about our project, please click [here](https://www.architecturelive.co.uk/projects/period-house-haslemere-surrey/) to read on.

Our clients have summarised their experience as follows:

_“It is hard to describe how much the resulting extension has changed our lives, indeed the word ‘extension’ doesn’t quite capture what we now live in. ArchitectureLIVE has changed our lives simply through supremely good, thoughtful and exciting design, by creating a totally unique space that reflects us as a family and provided a brand new living space”._

_Jim, Haslemere_

Voting closes on Friday, February 15th at 5pm.

Thank you for supporting us!
