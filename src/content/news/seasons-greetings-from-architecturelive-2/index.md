At the end of this extraordinary year, we, more than ever, would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to everyone we have had the pleasure to work with and who has supported our business and livelihoods.  

We are incredibly thankful that all our projects have been able to continue, progress and complete with even new construction sites starting up. We are also very pleased to welcome new projects to the fold. This is a real team effort, for consultants and contractors alike. 

This Christmas, our thoughts are with those families who have been adversely affected by the pandemic and we will therefore be supporting a local charity - [The Haslemere Food Bank](https://www.justgiving.com/crowdfunding/haslemerefoodbank). If you feel the same, please visit their website. 

We wish you and your family a very Merry Christmas and prosperous 2021!  
Jonathan, Irene & Kitty
