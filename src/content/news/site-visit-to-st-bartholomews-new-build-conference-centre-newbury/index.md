Building work is nearing completion at St Bartholomew’s School where a new conference centre provides the latest addition to the school's impressive facilities.

A new tiered seating auditorium with catering facilities and an ancillary meeting space will extend the provision for community and school events. 

The design for the new centre has been conceived as a distinctive, yet complementary element to the existing school, with rendered facades matching the existing colour concept and a new cantilevered canopy framing the new entrance to the 6th Form.  

A mural design developed by the school's current 6th Form students will further signpost the school's identity.
