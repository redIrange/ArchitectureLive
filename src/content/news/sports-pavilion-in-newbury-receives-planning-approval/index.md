We are delighted that planning permission has been granted for a new sports pavilion at Browns Meadow in Newbury.

The existing, WW2 timber pavilion is due to be demolished and requires replacement. Our design delivers a new, like-for-like single storey building which will provide modernised and accessible facilities for its users - Newbury Athletic Club and St Bartholomew’s School.
