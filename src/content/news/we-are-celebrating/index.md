We are absolutely delighted to have been ‘Highly Commended” in the Alterations & Extensions category for our extension project at the Waverley Design Awards last week.

Our design for a single storey rear and side extension to a detached home in Haslemere, was one of the smallest projects to be judged and was up against some very tough competition. At the award ceremony, the judges comments were that:-

_“… the scheme provided various surprises at different angles, whether it be the butterfly roof, turning to find the kitchen, or the glimpses seen from the street.”_ 

_“… the charm of this development is in its simplicity and detailing - it is an aspirational and achievable development that should stand out as a role model for other extensions throughout Waverley.”_

_“… overall, they were impressed with the extension and thought it worked very well on many levels, not least for living in”._

Congratulations to all our fellow winners, which you can see [here](https://www.waverley.gov.uk/downloads/file/7230/design_awards_2018_-_main_awards_winners).
